ELEMENT Group’s international energy trading arm actively trades crude oil, refined products, LNG and dry-bulk energy commodities

ELEMENT ALPHA

We provide the full suite of competitive logistics, financing and risk management services while sharing upside with our clients.

ELEMENT Alpha, a 100% subsidiary of ELEMENT Group SA, was established in 2015 and is headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland - the center of the international energy commodity trading community. ELEMENT aspires to become one of the world’s most competitive, reliable and admired providers of energy, logistics, financing and risk management services.

Our Business

ELEMENT Alpha trades all parts of the barrel from crude oil to light-ends, middle distillates and heavy ends as well as LNG and dry-bulk energy commodities. We have a deep understanding of the molecules we trade and excel at logistics and blending. Our multitude of skills in trade finance and risk management help create value for our suppliers and clients.
